Strategic Product Placement Analysis
Project Overview

This project analyzes how product placement inside a retail store impacts sales performance.
Using Tableau dashboards and data visualization, the project identifies patterns in product positioning, consumer demographics, pricing, and promotions.

A Flask-based web application is also developed to display the Tableau dashboards and story in an interactive website.

Problem Statement

Retail stores place products in different store locations such as aisles, end-caps, and front-of-store displays.
However, identifying which placement strategy generates the highest sales is a challenge.

This project analyzes product placement strategies and their impact on sales volume, pricing, and consumer demographics using Tableau visualizations.

Dataset Description

The dataset contains retail store sales and product placement information.

Key fields include:

Product Category

Product Position

Sales Volume

Price

Competitor Price

Promotion

Consumer Demographics

Season

This data helps analyze how different factors influence sales performance.

Dashboard Visualization

The Tableau dashboard contains multiple visualizations to analyze sales performance.

Main visualizations include:

Product Category Sales Comparison

Compares sales performance across categories such as Clothing, Electronics, and Food.

Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume

Shows how different consumer groups contribute to overall sales.

Competitor Price vs Price

Compares store product pricing with competitor pricing.

Seasonal Sales Analysis

Shows how sales vary across seasons for different product categories.

These visualizations help identify patterns in customer behavior and product performance.

Story Insights

The Tableau Story explains key insights derived from the dashboard:

Clothing category has the highest average sales volume.

Products placed at the front of the store generate higher sales.

High foot traffic areas increase product visibility and sales.

End-cap placements attract more customer attention.

Promotions influence product purchasing behavior.

Consumer demographics affect buying patterns.

Strategic product positioning improves sales performance.

Combining placement strategies with pricing and promotions maximizes revenue.
